Bienvenida/o a tu espacio

No son los eventos los que nos perturban, sino lo que pensamos sobre ellos.

La investigación en psicología clínica y neurociencia ha demostrado que el malestar emocional se origina en las creencias irracionales que construimos sobre lo que nos ocurre — no en los hechos en sí. Este es el fundamento científico de la Terapia Racional Emotivo Conductual (REBT), respaldada por décadas de evidencia empírica.

En terapia identificaremos esas creencias, las cuestionaremos con rigor y las reemplazaremos por formas de pensar más flexibles y funcionales — en un espacio seguro, confidencial y 100% online.

Las personas no se perturban por los hechos, sino por las opiniones que tienen de los hechos. — Epicteto

Welcome

It's not events that disturb us — it's what we tell ourselves about them.

Clinical psychology and neuroscience research has shown that emotional distress originates in the irrational beliefs we build around what happens to us — not in the events themselves. This is the scientific foundation of Rational Emotive Behavior Therapy (REBT), backed by decades of empirical evidence.

In therapy, we'll identify those beliefs, examine them rigorously, and replace them with more flexible and functional ways of thinking — in a safe, confidential, fully online space.

People are not disturbed by things, but by their opinions about things. — Epictetus

My background

How I work

I am a clinical psychologist certified in Rational Emotive Behavior Therapy (REBT) by the International Association IAREBT — the approach created by Albert Ellis, which holds that emotional distress comes not from events themselves, but from the irrational beliefs we build around them.

I am also a NIH-Fogarty scholarship recipient in the Master's Program in Neuroscience at Universidad Peruana Cayetano Heredia (UPCH) — NEUFIN Grant — which allows me to bring neuroscientific evidence about how the brain processes emotion directly into my clinical practice.
